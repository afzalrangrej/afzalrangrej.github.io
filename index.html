Excel Project

Bike Sales Dashboard

Built an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze customer purchasing behavior for bike sales. The dashboard uses pivot tables, slicers, and charts to explore relationships between income, age, commute distance, and bike purchases. Analysis revealed that middle-aged customers with shorter commute distances are more likely to purchase bikes, and higher income groups show stronger buying patterns. The dashboard allows dynamic filtering by marital status, education, region, and gender to uncover insights across customer segments.
